An exploration of the net.art movement

A short history of the net.art movement

  • 1984 - The movement begins with Eduardo Kac's "Reabracadabra"
  • 1990s - Other artists begin to create art solely for the Internet
  • Early 2000s - The original net.art movement comes to an end
  • 2005 onwards - Artists still create art using this technique, but it takes place under different names (such as post-internet art and new media art)
